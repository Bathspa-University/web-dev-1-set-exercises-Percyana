Vanilla Cupcake

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1/4 teaspoons baking powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt.
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter(softened)
  • 3/4 cup sugar.
  • 2 large eggs(room temperature)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk(or plain kefir, room temperature)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350° F(175°C).
  2. In the medium bowl, whisk together the flour,Baking powder, and salt.
  3. In separete large bowl cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  5. gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk.Begin and end with dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Line a Cupcake pan with cupcakes Lines.
  7. Fill each liner halfway with batter.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick, inserted into center comes out clean.
  9. Let the cupcakes cool completely before frosting

frosting (Optional)

You can frost these cupcake with your favourite vanilla buttercream or cream cheese frosting.Sprinkle with colorful sprinkles for an extra touch!
